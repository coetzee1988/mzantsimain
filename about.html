Who We Are

Mzantsi Solutions is leading procurement and servicing centre that specializes in Airconditioning, refrigeration, lighting as well as FMCG with a footprint across Sub-Saharan Africa.

Mzantsi Solutions offers you a full 360 solution for any project and procurement need. Not only do we ensure your facility is equipped with top quality lighting, air conditioning and refrigeration equipment, we offer a unique service of fully supplying your coolers, cold rooms and shelfs with some of Africa’s leading consumables and brands.
